About Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist and former chairman of Tata Sons. He is widely respected for his leadership, ethics, and contribution to society.

Achievements

  • Former Chairman of Tata Group
  • Recipient of Padma Bhushan (2000)
  • Recipient of Padma Vibhushan (2008)
  • Expanded Tata Group globally

Contributions

Ratan Tata is known for his philanthropic work in education, healthcare, and rural development. Under his leadership, Tata Group acquired global brands like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley.
